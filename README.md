App Store Description

Pop. Flip. Repeat.

digiPop is the most satisfying digital fidget toy you'll find. Modeled after the iconic bubble wrap pop toy, digiPop lets you tap, drag, and work your way through boards of squishy bubbles — each one delivering a crisp haptic pop that feels just like the real thing.

Fidget your way
Choose from a growing collection of board shapes — compact grids, wide layouts, honeycomb patterns, and more — each unlocking as you rack up pops. Flip any board to pop from the back side and start fresh from a new angle.

Make it yours
Customise your experience with multiple sound profiles (Classic, Xylo, Droid, and more), color schemes, and board designs. Unlock everything simply by popping — no purchases required.

Feels amazing
Every bubble delivers precision haptic feedback tuned to your chosen sound profile. Pop one, pop a hundred — it never gets old.

Track your progress
Watch your pop count climb, clear boards, earn achievements, and unlock new content as your collection grows.

Pure and simple
No timers. No scores to beat. No pressure. Just satisfying, stress-relieving pops whenever you need them.

Great for kids, adults, fidgeters, focus sessions, or just something to do with your hands.
